Information

We are a group of Electronic Engineering students from the Polytechnic University of Valencia who want to start up a company in order to fill a gap in the market. One of us controls the informatic part of this product, other members have experience in the sales department and all of us have worked together in the past, so can assure that we will have a good working environment.

Our business idea is the design of an app that will inform the user’s emergency contacts and the authorities if they are in any kind of danger or risk, such as sex abuse, rape or hate towards the LGBT+ community, among others.

The company, even though it will be oriented towards the digital market and, therefore, distributed worldwide, it is located in the city of Valencia, Spain.
